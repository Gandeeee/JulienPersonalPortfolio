About me

I am Julien Gallardo, a versatile and passionate model, ready to captivate and collaborate. With a unique blend of creativity and dedication, I invite you to explore a curated collection of my work that showcases my adaptability, style, and commitment to the craft. Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of collaborating with brands such as MANbyManikan, KURA-KURA Beer, Fender Ferd Official, and walked the runway in Jakarta for JF3 Semifinals 2023. You might also recognize me from my role in Season 2 of the TV show 'Turn On,' where I had the incredible opportunity to bring a character to life. Join me as we dive into a collection that reflects not only my past experiences but also the endless possibilities that lie ahead in the world of modeling.
